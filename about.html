Our story

Fourteen Years in the Making

From one outreach program to a network of initiatives spanning education, skills, and humanitarian relief — this is the record of how Hadassah Foundation came to be.

How it began

A Small Promise, Kept and Grown

Hadassah Foundation was founded in 2012 with a simple conviction: that dignity, opportunity, and hope should not depend on circumstance. What began as informal support for a handful of families in Lagos has, over fourteen years, become a structured foundation serving thousands across multiple states — built one program, one partnership, one volunteer at a time.

Mission

Transforming Lives Through Compassionate Service

To restore hope and create lasting opportunities for vulnerable individuals and communities through education, skills development, humanitarian relief, and compassionate outreach.

Vision

A World Where Every Life Has the Chance to Flourish

We envision communities where every person — regardless of circumstance — has access to the support, skills, and opportunities needed to build a dignified, self-sufficient life.

Meet our founder

A Message From Our Founder

Esther Oluwafeyisayo Olafisoye

Founder & Executive Director
Hadassah Initiative for Empowerment Foundation

Esther Oluwafeyisayo Olafisoye is the visionary founder of Hadassah Initiative for Empowerment Foundation, driven by an unwavering passion for humanitarian service and social transformation. Her journey was shaped by a deep conviction that lasting change begins with empowering those most often overlooked — women striving for economic independence, children in need of educational opportunity, and communities seeking a path toward self-sufficiency.

Under her leadership, the foundation has grown into a trusted platform for women empowerment, youth development, and community outreach, touching thousands of lives through skills training, education support, and humanitarian relief. Esther leads with compassion and conviction, believing that true leadership means walking alongside the people you serve — listening first, then building solutions that last. Her commitment to creating positive social impact continues to shape every program the foundation delivers.
